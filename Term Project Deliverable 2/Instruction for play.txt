Instruction:

1. Open TP2.py in the Demo. 
2. Use "up" or "down" key to select "Play," "Help," etc.
3. Choose the background for battlefield you want
4. RIGHT click to choose the level package: 1-3, 4-6.
	For other buttons (say return the main menu), use LEFT click.
5. Enter the level number you want, and hit "ENTER" key ("Return" key).
	If the level you choose is unavailable, the game will tell you so.
6. Save the progress (the level you have completed) at any time by pressing "s."
	Read the record by pressing "space" key.
7. Rules for play are in the "Help."

Thank you!
